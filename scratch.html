Kobe Bryant

Kobe Bryant was one of the greatest basketball players to ever play in the NBA. He played for the Los Angeles Lakers for 20 years and amassed 33,643 points, good for 4th most in NBA history. Kobe was born on August 23, 1978 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He was selected with the 13th pick by the Charlotte Hornets in the 1996 NBA Draft and was traded to the Lakers that same night. He married his wife Vanessa in 2001 and had two daughets, Gianna and Natalia. On January 26, 2020, Kobe and his daughter Gianna tragically passed away in a helicopter accident that shocked the whole world. This page is in honor and rememberance of one of the NBA's greats, Kobe Bryant.
